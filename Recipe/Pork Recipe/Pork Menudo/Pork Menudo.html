Ingredients

Name Quantity
Pork Shoulder(sliced) 2 lbs
Knorr Pork Cube 1 pieces
Pork liver (cubed) 4 ounces
Baking potato (cubed) 1 pieces
Hotdogs (sliced crosswise into thin pieces) 3 pieces
Tomato sauces 8 oz. 1 can
Carrot cubed 1 piece
Raisins 1/2 cup
Dried bay leaves 2 pieces
Soy sauce 2 tablespoons
Ground ginger 1 teaspoon
Yellow onion chopped 1 piece
Garlic Crushed 4 cloves
Cooking Oil 3 tablespoon
Water 2 to 4 cups

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Once the gets hot, Start to pan fry the potato and carrots. Continue until sides turns light brown. Set aside.
  2. Heat the remaining oil(add more if needed). Add the pork liver and then stir fry from 30 seconds. Add ground ginger. Continue to stir-fry for 2 minutes. Set aside.
  3. Pour 3 tablespoons of cooking oil into a cooking pot. Once the oil turns hot, saute garlic and onion.
  4. Once the onion becomes soft and translucent, add the pork. Saute until light brown.
  5. Put the hotdogs into the pot. Cook for 1 minute.
  6. Pour soy sauce, tomato sauce, and water into the pot.
  7. Add Knorr Pork Cube. Stir.
  8. Add raisins and dried bay leaves. Stir. Cover the pot and continue to boil.
  9. Add the pan fried potato and carrots, liver, along with salt and ground black pepper. Stir and cook for 3 minutes.
  10. Transfer to a serving plate. Serve.
