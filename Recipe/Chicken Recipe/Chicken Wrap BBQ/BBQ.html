BBQ Chicken Wrap

Ingredients

Name Quantity
Boneless chicken breast 2 4 oz. pieces
Salt 1 teaspoon
Vegetable oil 1/2 teaspoons
BBQ sauce (homemade or store bought) 1/2 cup
Sharp chaddar cheese 1/2 cup
Large flour tortillas 2 pieces
Chopped fresh romaine lettuce 2 cups
Chopped purple cabbage 1/4 cup
Chopped green cabbage 3/4 cup
Carrots (julienne) 1/4 cup

Steps

  1. Rub salt all over the chicken breast. Let it stay for 10 minutes.
  2. Coat the chicken with vegetable oil.
  3. Heat-up the grill. Start to grill one side of the chicken for 3 minutes per side.
  4. Paste BBQ sauce on each side of the chicken and continue to grill for another 3 to 4 minutes per side. Remove from the grill and let it cool down.
  5. Slice the grilled chickedn BBQ crosswise into strips. Apply the remaining BBQ sauce on top.
  6. Meanwhile, lay a piece of flour tortilla flat on a large plate. Arrange half of each fresh vegetables over the tortilla. Top with cheese and slices of chicken from the breast.
  7. Secure the contents by wrapping the tortilla all over it. Do this by folding a quarter of both sides(left and right) inwards. Roll the flour tortilla from bottom to top until contents are secured
  8. Serve with favorite dressing.
