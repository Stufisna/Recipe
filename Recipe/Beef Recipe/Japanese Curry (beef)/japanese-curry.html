Ingredients

Name Quantity
beef (thinly sliced) 2 lbs.
Knorr beef cube 1 piece
potatoes (diced) 2 pieces
carrots (diced) 2 pieces
Curry powder 4 tablespoons
tomato ketchup 3 tablespoons
Worcestershire sauce 3 tablespoons
Honey 3 teaspoons
yellow onions (chopped) 2 pieces
garlic (sliced) 3 cloves
Water 3 cups
Cornstarch 1 tablespoon
Coffee ½ cup
Cooking oil 2 tablespoons
Butter 1 tablespoon
Cheddar cheese ¼ cup grated

Steps

  1. Season beef with salt and pepper. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a cooking pot. Add the butter and let it melt.
  3. Put the beef slices and stir-fry until it turns light brown in color.
  4. Add onion and garlic. Sauté for 1 minute.
  5. Add the carrot and cook it for 2 minutes while stirring occasionally.
  6. Pour the water into the cooking pot. Let it boil.
  7. Add the Knorr Beef Cube, Curry Powder, Ketchup. Worcestershire sauce, and honey. Cover and Simmer for 15 minutes.
  8. Add the potato and continue to simmer for 12 minutes.
  9. Combine cornstarch with 3 tablespoons of water. Stir and pour into the pot. Stir.
  10. Add cheese and coffee.
  11. Season with salt and ground black pepper as needed.
