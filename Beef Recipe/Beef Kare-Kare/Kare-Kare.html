Ingredients

Name Quantity
Ox tail 1 1/2 lb.
Beef tripe 1 lb.
Knorr Beef Cube 1 pieces
Pechay 1 bunch
Talong (sliced) 1 piece
Sitaw (cut into 2 inch pieces) 12 pieces
Banana blossoms 1 cup sliced
Peanut butter 3/4 cup
Annatto seeds 1/2 cup
Bagoong alamang 1/4 cup
Cornstarch 1 teaspoon
Yellow onion 1 piece
Garlic (crushed) 4 cloves
Cooking oil 3 tablespoons
Water 2 quarts

Steps

  1. Boil quart water in a pressure cooker. Add oxtail and tripe. Continue to boil in medium heat for 20 minutes. Note: do not cover the cooker.
  2. Drain the water use to boil the beef. Pour 1 quart of fresh water into the pressure cooker. Cover and pressure cook for 25 minutes.
  3. Prepare annatto water by combining 1 cup of hot water with annatto seed. Stir. Soak seeds for 10 minutes. Remove the seeds by filtering using a kitchen sieve. Set aside.
  4. Heat oil in a cooking pot. Saute onion and garlic until onion softens.
  5. Pour beef stock into the pot. Note: this is the liquid used to pressure cook the oxtail and tripe. Let boil.
  6. Add peanut butter. Stir until well blended.
  7. Add Knorr Beef Cube. Stir.
  8. Pour Annatto water into the pot and then add oxtail and beef tripe. Let the mixture re-boil. Cook for 5 to 7 minutes.
  9. Combine cornstarch with 1/4 cup. Stir. Pour into the cooking pot. Stir.
